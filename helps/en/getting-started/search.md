# Search in the workspace

Search text across files in the open folder from the left sidebar.

## How to open

1. [Open a folder](open-project.md)
2. Click the **Search** tab at the top of the left sidebar (next to **Explorer**)

Search is disabled until a folder is open.

## What you can do

- Search across workspace files
- Optional **replace** (toggle in the Search panel)
- Match options: case sensitive, whole word, regular expression
- Include / exclude globs to narrow the scope
- Click a match to open the file at that line

## Tips

- Large folders may take a moment; progress shows while searching
- Ignored folders such as `node_modules` and `.git` are skipped, same as Explorer
